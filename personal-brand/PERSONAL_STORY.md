# Personal Brand Story - Matthew Scott

**Purpose**: Humanize the portfolio, build trust through story (not resume)

---

## Core Story (Who I Am)

**I believe in transparency and thoroughness.**

When I discovered small AI models couldn't do math reliably, I didn't hide it - I built hybrid systems. When projects got archived hastily and had to be recovered, I learned to give everything a fair shake before deciding.

This approach - honest about limitations, thorough in investigation, transparent in process - is how I work.

---

## Journey (How I Got Here)

**From traditional development to AI-native**:
- Started building systems the standard way (write all code myself)
- Discovered AI could scaffold 85% of code (if I QA'd the other 15%)
- Realized: Orchestrating AI > Writing code manually (new category of work)

**From building everything to focusing on validation**:
- Assessed 40 projects, looking for what to deploy
- Discovered: I already had capabilities with pilot data (Norton, personal use traction)
- Learned: Validation matters more than volume (3 proven > 40 unproven)

**From infrastructure to execution**:
- Built deployment tools (Oracle, Elite Frontend) to assess and build faster
- Realized: Tools support strategy, don't replace it
- Focused: Use tools to execute, not keep building tools

**The pattern**: Learn, adapt, focus on what actually matters (outcomes, not activity)

---

## Values (What Drives Decisions)

**Truth over comfort**: I'll tell you AI can't do arithmetic (and show the hybrid fix)
**Thoroughness over speed**: Fair shake investigation prevents costly mistakes (Archive-Recovery lesson)
**Privacy-first over convenience**: Local processing = compliance (Mirador's advantage)
**Transparency over authority**: Show the process, don't claim expertise without proof

**These aren't aspirational.** They're how I actually work (documented in 8 principles).
